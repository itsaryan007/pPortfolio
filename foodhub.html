Foodhub Application

During my time at Bigfoot Infotech, I had a chance to work on the project of Foodhub. I worked on the complete design process from discovery, definition, ideation, design and user testing for the project.

The Food Hub app is a mobile application designed to help users discover new places to eat and explore various food options in their vicinity. The app provides a user-friendly interface that allows users to search for restaurants based on their desired cuisine and access information about the available menu items. Additionally, users can create a personalized profile, save their favorite restaurants, and place orders directly through the app.
